Seattle Quick Tour

See, Experience, Eat

Seattle, Founded in 1851

So you only have one day to tour Seattle and you want to see the city highlights.

Your day will be jam packed but not to worry as you will be fueled with excitement and wonder as you see all that Seattle has to offer.

Without futher ado, let's jump right into it!

Top 5 Sights

  • Pike Place Market: World Famous Flying Fish
  • Seattle Water Front: Have stroll along the waterfront and visit some eclectic shops
  • Space Needle: A must visit for any first time visitor - take a ride up or wait and view Seattle from a rooftop bar
  • South Lake Union: During the summer months wander around Lake Union and watch all the actiity on the lake
  • View Seattle from a Rooftop: From Astra hotel in South Lake Union take the elevator to the rooftop bar - free to get to the bar

Top 5 Eats

  • World Famous Mac & Cheese Beechers @ Pike Place Market
  • Mini Donuts Daily Dozen Doughnut Co. @ Pike Place Market
  • Seafood Fish & Chips at @ Ivars Water Front
  • Pho Soup Ahn Noodle House @ Lower Queen Anne
  • Artisnal Ramen Ooink @ Capital Hill

Schedule

Pike Place Market - 9:00am - 10:30am

Start you day off with a visit the the iconic pike place market. Head straight to the original Starbucks. Located at the northend of the market. Get your caffeine to fuel you up for your day.

Wander through the market and visit various vendor stalls. As you make your way down towards the famous flying fish market. Grab some World Famous Mac & Cheese from Beechers small size is all you need (suppoesdly Oprah once stopped by an claimed it was the world best mac & cheese, recommeneded to get half spicy and half non-spicy off the request but they get it so often that they know what your asking for). Then Grab a dozen donuts from the daily dozen donut vendor stall located across from the flying fish vendor stall.

Then wander down to the famously disgusting Gum Wall located outside and below the flying fish vendor.

Seattle Water Front - 11:00am - 12:00pm

From Pike Place Market continue walking down to the water front, it's a well marked walking path

Visit some of the eclectic shops along the water front. The shop to to be missed is the Ye'old Curiosity Shop. Look around while in the shop you will see interesting items every where you look

Contiue walking along the water front and taking in the sights. If you have extra time why not take a ferry ride to bremerton and back. Its a great way to see Seattle from the water.

Space Needle - 1:00pm - 1:30pm

No trip is complete with a visit to the Space Needle. Built in ____ for the worlds fair. A true architectural gem. A building so iconic that you instantly know your in Seattle.

Ramen 2:30pm - 3:30pm

A trip to Seattle is not complete with out a bowl of noodles. It probaly rained during your trip so now it time to warm up with a bowl of noodles

My recommnedation is to get Ramen Noodles at Ooink - they have a few different kinds noodles but all are fantastic. If you need a close location head to ahn noodle a short walk from the space needle and get some fantastic Vietnames Pho Soup all are good but the combination has everything tell its your first time and you had recommendaiton, they will walk you through what to order.
